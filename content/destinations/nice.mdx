Nice is the Riviera's working city — glamorous but lived-in, French but almost Italian, a place where morning markets matter as much as evening yachts. The Promenade des Anglais curves along the Baie des Anges, locals play pétanque in Cimiez, and every restaurant claims the true salade niçoise.

The lifestyle is promenade walks, market lunches, and the particular pleasure of doing very little in extraordinary surroundings. Nice doesn't demand performance — it invites you to sit down, order a coffee, and watch the light change over the sea.
